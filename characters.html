Main Characters

Spongebob Squarepants

Spongebob is the main character of the show. He is a sea sponge with a happy-go-lucky personality, and lives in a pineapple under the sea with his pet snail, Gary. His best friend is Patrick.

Patrick Star

Patrick is another main character and Spongebob's best friend. He is a sea star that lives under a rock. He is often considered naive, but he has a good heart.

Gary

Garold "Gary" Wilson Jr. is Spongebob's pet snail. He is a domesticated pet whose mannerisms resemble that of a house cat.

Mr. Krabs

Mr. Krabs owns the Krusty Krab restaurant, where Spongebob and Squidward work. He is a red crab that is obsessed with money and often puts money ahead of his employees and his own daughter.

Squidward Tentacles

Squidward is the octopus neighbor of Spongebob and Patrick, and the cashier at the Krusty Krab. He is a talented artist and musician, but has a very cynical and selfish personality. He is often annoyed by Spongebob and Patrick.

Mrs. Puff

Mrs. Puff is a pufferfish and Spongebob's Boating School teacher with a mysterious past. She often appears paranoid and anxious, especially in regards to Spongebob's driving.

Sandy Cheeks

Sandy is a squirrel from the state of Texas. She is incredibly intelligent and physically strong, as evidenced in several episodes of the show. As a scientist, she originally came to Bikini Bottom to study sea creatures, but often misses her home.

Sheldon Plankton

Sheldon J. Plankton, more well known as simply, Plankton, is a planktonic copepod who runs the Chum Bucket, a failing restaurant. Plankton and his wife, Karen, a waterproof computer, are the main antagonists of the show, as they are often attempting to steal the secret Krabby Patty formula to bring success to their business.
